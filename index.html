GirlUp TIET is a community dedicated to accelerating the movement for gender equality. We train, inspire, and connect girls to be leaders and changemakers.

Our Core Values

Leadership

We nurture the skills needed to lead local and global change for a more equitable world.

Community

Building a supportive network where every voice is heard, valued, and empowered.

Equality

Advocating for justice and equal opportunities for women in education, health, and beyond.

Our Initiatives

Projects driven by the GirlUp TIET chapter to create lasting impact on campus.

Tech-Equality

A series of technical workshops aimed at increasing female participation in engineering and computer science.

Campus Voices

A student-led advocacy program focused on safety and gender-neutral facilities across the TIET campus.

Sisterhood Support

Peer-to-peer mentoring linking senior students with newcomers for career guidance and academic help.
