Our Initiatives

Projects driven by the GirlUp TIET chapter to create lasting impact on campus.

School Drive

Our Girl Up Society organized a school drive to raise awareness that menstruation is not a taboo, while also distributing sanitary pads to young girls in a government college..

Flaunt Your Flaws

Flaunt Your Flaws, organized by Girl Up TIET Society aims at celebrating individuality and encouraging participants to embrace their imperfections with confidence. Through creative activities and engaging discussions, the event highlighted how flaws are not shortcomings but unique aspects of one’s identity. The event provides a safe and inclusive space where authenticity was celebrated, aligning with Girl Up’s mission of empowering individuals to be proud of who they are.

Noor

Noor is an ideathon organized by the Girl Up Society, offering a platform for young minds to present impactful ideas on feminism and gender equality. The event aimed to inspire innovation while fostering dialogue on social change.
